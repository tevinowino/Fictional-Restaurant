Savory Kenyan Delights Menu

Appetizers

  • Samosa

    Crispy pastry filled with spiced minced meat or vegetables, served with a tangy dipping sauce.

  • Mutura

    Traditional Kenyan sausage made from a mixture of meat, spices, and blood, grilled to perfection.

  • Kachumbari

    Fresh tomato and onion salad, garnished with coriander and a splash of lemon juice.

Main Courses

  • Nyama Choma

    Grilled meat, typically goat or beef, seasoned with salt and served with a side of Ugali and Sukuma Wiki.

  • Chicken Biryani

    Aromatic rice dish cooked with spiced chicken, tomatoes, and a blend of traditional Kenyan spices.

  • Githeri

    A hearty mix of boiled maize and beans, often seasoned with onions, tomatoes, and spices.

Desserts

  • Mahamri

    Sweet, fried dough flavored with coconut milk and cardamom, a popular coastal treat.

  • Mukimo

    Mashed potatoes mixed with pumpkin leaves, maize, and peas, often served as a side or on its own.

  • Mandazi

    Soft, sweet, and fluffy doughnuts, lightly spiced with cardamom and coconut milk.
